Prerequisitorys:
Gimp
A tool to screenshot with
A top panel that is 19-20 rows high
A bottom dock that is 33-34 rows high

Step 1: Use XFCE
Step 2: Set the background of the top panel to toppanel.png
Step 3: Install gimp and screenshot your desktop
Step 4: Open the screenshot of your desktop in gimp and crop the image to the exact size of the bottom pane.
Step 5: Combine the endbit and middle in gimp, the endbit should be at the end and the middle should be stretched to the edge.
Step 6: Set the bottom panel to the image you just made.
